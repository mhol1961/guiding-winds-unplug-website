Our Story

Meet Clint & Dodie.

Clint was born in Miami Beach and raised in the Florida Keys. The ocean wasn't a backdrop — it was a way of life. He spent years as a long-liner before he ever guided a guest, and he knows these waters the way most people know their own neighborhoods.

Dodie grew up between Fort Lauderdale and Pompano Beach. She shares Clint's love for the sea — and adds something else: a quiet belief that the ocean is one of the most direct paths back to yourself. She runs the wellness side of every charter and is the reason "Unplug" is in the name.

Together they've spent years cruising the British Virgin Islands and fell in love with the catamaran lifestyle and the freedom it offers. Guiding Winds is the natural next step — a way to share that freedom with eight to twelve people at a time.
