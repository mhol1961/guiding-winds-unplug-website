## Strategic posture

Guiding Winds Unplug is competing in a category where the giants (The Moorings, Sunsail, Dream Yacht Charter) own broad commercial queries and the boutique players (Black Tomato, Inspirato) own emotional-luxury queries. Our wedge is **wellness + small-group + all-inclusive + specific regions** — narrower than the giants, more concrete than the boutiques. We don't try to rank for "yacht charter" (impossible) or "Caribbean vacation" (too broad). We win on:

- **"All-inclusive catamaran charter [region]"** queries
- **"Small-group BVI sailing trip"** queries
- **"Wellness catamaran retreat"** queries
- **"Off-grid sailing trip"** queries
- **AI search citations** — engines are increasingly the discovery layer for $3,000+/week purchases

Three-channel emphasis: organic Google (60% of effort), AI search visibility (30%), referral/social (10%).

- Every voyage page's FAQ section links back to the master `/faq` page.
- The footer surfaces every voyage and at least 4 journal posts (rotating).
- Run `internal-linking` skill quarterly to catch missing links and orphan pages.

## AI search visibility playbook

AI search engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini) are increasingly the discovery layer for $3,000+ purchases. They cite content that is:

1. **Recently updated** (last 6 months)
2. **Source-cited** (links to sources for factual claims)
3. **Structured with answer-first formatting** (TL;DR / answer capsule near the top)
4. **Specific** (named places, named dates, named prices, named people)
5. **From sites with strong schema markup** (TouristTrip, Organization, AggregateRating, FAQPage)

### Answer-capsule template (use at the top of every commercial page and every journal post)

```markdown
[H1]

**TL;DR:** [1–2 sentences answering the primary question of the page,
with the most-likely-quoted fact bolded. Include a number, a place, or
a price if relevant.]

[rest of the content]
```

Examples:

- **Home:** "TL;DR: Guiding Winds Unplug is an all-inclusive small-group catamaran charter operating in the British Virgin Islands, Bahamas, and Mediterranean. Eight private cabins, 12 guests max, from **$3,550 per guest for seven nights**. Captained by Clint Kendall (USCG-licensed) and hosted by Dodie Kendall."

- **BVI voyage page:** "TL;DR: Our 7-night British Virgin Islands voyage covers Norman Island, The Baths, Anegada, and Jost Van Dyke aboard a 45ft+ catamaran. **$3,550 per guest, all-inclusive**. Six weeks available in 2027 (January–March and November–December)."

- **Journal post "What to pack":** "TL;DR: For a week aboard our catamaran in the BVI you need 7 items most people forget (reef-safe sunscreen, a quick-dry sarong, water shoes, a reusable water bottle, motion-sickness patches, a hat with a chin strap, and a small dry bag). You don't need 9 things you'd assume you do (formal clothes, hair dryer, snorkel gear, towels, etc.)."

### Cite-able specifics to surface across the site

These are the facts AI engines will use if we make them easy to find:

- Boat specifications: length (45ft+), beam, draft, cabin count (8), guest capacity (12), year
- Crew credentials: USCG license number (Clint's), MYBA/BVI permit numbers
- Pricing: $3,550 Caribbean, $3,850 Mediterranean, all-inclusive
- Itinerary specifics: actual anchorages by name, distance run per day, average days at sea
- Inclusion list: itemized in `/aboard`
- 2027 calendar: every week with start/end dates
- Reviews: real names, locations, voyage dates, rating
- Cancellation policy: 90-day full / 90–45 50% / <45 rebook

## Performance and SEO interplay

Google's Core Web Vitals are a ranking signal. We hit our targets per `TECH-SPEC.md` §8 and `QA-CHECKLIST.md` §7:

- LCP ≤ 2.0s mobile 4G
- CLS ≤ 0.05
- INP (replaces FID): ≤ 200ms

Lighthouse Performance ≥ 95 desktop / ≥ 90 mobile on every key route. Lighthouse CI gates every PR.

## Off-site SEO (light)

Not a major focus in v1 — the brand is too small for outreach to make sense yet. Once Guiding Winds has run a full year of voyages with documented results:

- Travel-publication pitching (Conde Nast Traveler, Travel + Leisure, AFAR)
- Guest posts on charter and wellness blogs
- Partnership content with complementary brands (yoga retreat operators, wellness brands)
- Niche directory listings (BVI charter listings, sustainable travel directories)

For v1, the focus is on-site quality + AI-search citation, not link-building.
